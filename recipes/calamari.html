Fried Calamari

Calamari battered in a flour mixture, fried to golden perfection

Ingredients
  • 1 pound of Calamari tubes, cleaned
  • Oil for frying
  • 6 cups of all purpose flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground pepper
  • 1 wedge of lemon
Steps
  1. In a deep fryer or large saucepan, heat enough oil to 400 degrees F
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk eggs. In a seperate medium sized bowl, combine flour, salt, and paprika
  3. Toss calamari rings in eggs, then toss in the flour mixture until completely coated
  4. Submerge battered calamari in heated oil for 3-4 minutes, or until golden brown. Do this in batches to prevent overcrowding in the pan
  5. After removing calamari from oil, place on paper towels for oil to drip off. Serve with lemon wedge
