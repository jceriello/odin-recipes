Tiramisu

Coffee soaked ladyfingers, layered with sweet cream

Ingredients
  • 1 cup of espresso
  • 1 package of ladyfingers
  • 6 egg yolks
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 2 cups of heavy cream
  • 1 1/4 cups of mascarpone
  • Cocoa powder, for decorating
Steps
  1. Bring a medium saucepan with water to a boil, then reduce heat to medium. Place a glass bowl over the pan, and add eggs and sugar
  2. Stir constantly for 10 minutes, until the egg and sugar combines and turns to lemon-yellow color
  3. Remove from heat and allow to cool to room temperature, then mix in the mascarpone until combined
  4. Using a mixer, whip heavy cream until stiff peaks are formed, then fold the egg and mascarpone mixture into the cream
  5. Soak ladyfingers for about a second in the espresso, then form a layer of them side by side in a 13x9 dish
  6. After forming first layer, spread half the cream mixture, then repeat for another layer, and spread the rest. Sprinkle cocoa powder on top, set in fridge for at least 4 hours.
